Academic Background

I’ve built a strong foundation in full-stack web development through a diverse set of college-level courses that emphasized hands-on learning and industry-relevant skills. Starting with core fundamentals in programming logic and web systems, I progressed into advanced client-side and backend technologies that allowed me to craft dynamic, scalable applications. In courses like CS 2350 and WEB 2700, I explored server configuration, RESTful APIs, authentication methods, and database integration—gaining practical experience that mirrors real-world development challenges.

On the frontend, I developed a deep understanding of responsive design, user experience, and modern JavaScript frameworks through classes like CS 3350 and WEB 1430. These courses sharpened my ability to build sleek interfaces that are intuitive and accessible. My education also emphasized teamwork, Git version control, and agile development workflows, especially during my full-stack project in WEB 2890. Altogether, this academic journey reflects my commitment to writing clean, efficient code and delivering complete web solutions from concept to deployment.

Professional Experience

My career began in the construction world, where I learned the value of grit, precision, and perseverance from the ground up. Starting out in a demanding field taught me how to navigate tough environments, communicate clearly, and lead by example. I rose through the ranks—from laborer to operator, and now foreman—not just by mastering tools and techniques, but by showing up every day with a mindset focused on progress. That experience shaped how I approach challenges, manage teams, and deliver results, no matter the industry. It built my confidence and my leadership style—practical, honest, and grounded—and became a springboard for everything I've pursued since.

Personal Interests

When I'm not on the job site or behind a screen, I'm all about spending time outdoors and making memories with the people I care about. I’ve got a passion for hunting small game, usually in marshland swamps or wide open country—quiet places that demand patience, sharp instincts, and a good set of boots. Fishing is another favorite, especially when I’m out there with my buddies or sharing the experience with my son; there’s something special about a calm morning on the water and the stories that come with it. I’m also big on family road trips, whether it’s chasing scenic views or tracking down the best roadside diner. Attending rodeos is a tradition I keep close—it’s got that raw energy, community spirit, and just enough dust to feel like home. Bonfire nights with the guys and family are a staple—sharing food, laughs, and stories under a starry sky. And when the mood strikes, I enjoy shooting skeet, keeping my aim sharp and soaking up that rush of competition and focus. When it’s time to wind down, I’m all in for movie nights and the occasional shopping trip with my wife—which, let’s be honest, ends up being more fun than I expected every time.
